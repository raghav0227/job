Engineering jobs are all about applying scientific principles to solve problems and create things. Engineers use their knowledge of math, physics, and other sciences to design, develop, build, and test a wide variety of things, from tiny circuits to massive bridges.

  • Design: This involves coming up with ideas for new products, systems, or processes. Engineers will consider things like functionality, safety, and cost during this stage.
  • Devlop: Once a design is finalized, engineers turn it into a reality. This may involve creating prototypes, writing technical instructions, or overseeing construction.
  • Build: Many engineers are involved in the physical creation of things. This could involve working on assembly lines, managing construction projects, or installing equipment.
    There are many different types of engineering
  • Civil Engineering: Deals with designing and building infrastructure, like roads, bridges, and dams.
  • Mechanical Engineering: Focuses on designing and building machines, from engines to robots..
  • Electrical Engineering: Works with electricity and electronics to create things like power grids and computers.
  • Chemical Engineering: Applies chemistry to design processes for things like creating new materials or refining oil.
